Intern

Job 8

Job 9

Job 10

About STX Movies

About Us

STX Movies is a global entertainment company dedicated to producing, financing, marketing, and distributing high-quality motion pictures and digital content. The company focuses on creating engaging stories that connect with audiences around the world, working with talented filmmakers, actors, and creative professionals.


Through innovation and a commitment to original storytelling, STX Movies has delivered a variety of successful films across multiple genres, providing audiences with memorable entertainment experiences both in theaters and through digital platforms.

Movie Production & Distribution

STX Movies specializes in developing and distributing feature films for global audiences. The company collaborates with creative talent and production partners to bring compelling stories to life, ensuring high production standards and wide-reaching distribution.


Its portfolio includes action, comedy, drama, thriller, and family-oriented films, helping establish STX Movies as a recognized name in the entertainment industry.
